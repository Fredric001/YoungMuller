Our Story

In the charming Country of Kenya, skilled carpenter Kelvin founded "ArtisanWood Creations" with a vision for bespoke, handcrafted furniture. From a modest workshop, the company quickly gained a reputation for quality craftsmanship and personalized service. Embracing sustainability, they expanded to offer cabinetry, trim installations, and renovations. The studio became a hub for creativity, combining traditional values with modern technology. ArtisanWood Creations not only shaped beautiful furniture but also impacted the community through education and charitable initiatives, leaving a lasting legacy in Kenya and beyond.
